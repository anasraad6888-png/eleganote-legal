What is Eleganote?

Eleganote is a note-taking application for writing, drawing, organizing documents, and keeping your work on your device by default. You can optionally connect Google Drive or Dropbox to store encrypted backup ZIP files in a dedicated folder named Eleganote Backups.

  • Handwriting, shapes, text, tables, and images
  • Audio notes with optional recording
  • Local-first storage — your notes stay on your device
  • Optional cloud backup to Google Drive or Dropbox
  • Export and share notes as PDF or backup archives
